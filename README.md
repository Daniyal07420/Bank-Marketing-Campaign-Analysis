# 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end analysis of a real-world **Bank Marketing Campaign Dataset** using **Python** and **Power BI**.

The objective is to identify customer behavior, evaluate marketing campaign performance, and discover the key factors influencing customer subscriptions for term deposits.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow, including data cleaning, feature engineering, exploratory data analysis (EDA), business analysis, KPI reporting, and dashboard development.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Banks invest significant resources in telemarketing campaigns to promote financial products.

The challenge is identifying:

- Which customers are most likely to subscribe?
- Which marketing strategies perform better?
- Which customer characteristics influence campaign success?

This project answers these questions through data-driven analysis.

---

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Customers with secondary education represent the largest customer segment.
- Marketing performance varies across different months.
- Contact method significantly impacts campaign effectiveness.
- Housing loan ownership influences customer segmentation.
- Previous campaign outcomes provide valuable insights for future campaigns.
